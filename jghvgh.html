A Briefly History of Halabja

Halabja is a city in the kurdstan region of iraq,serving as the capital of halabja Governorate.it is located approximatly 240 km northeast of Baghdad,The city is historically significant due to the Halabja massage, wich occured on March 16,1988 when thousands of Kurds were killed by a large-scale chemical attack carried out by the iraqi government.
